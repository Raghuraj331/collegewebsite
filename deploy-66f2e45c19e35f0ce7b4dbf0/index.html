Message By Chairman

Dr. Tej Bahadur Singh

In the Memory of my beloved parents and with the blessing and grace of Parampita Parmatma, after serving the entire region" as a Doctor and being from Medical background, I feel much honored and fortunate to lead and step into the field of education by setting up a Technical Institute Approved By PCI and All India Council Of Technical Education as our first step towards serving the nation and the students. Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial College of Pharmacy duly run by Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial Educational Foundation Trust. India is emerging out to be true leader in the field of research and Pharmaceuticals. Quality of medicines manufactured in India is proven to be of world class standard and many Indian Pharmaceutical companies have taken a lead to capture world pharmaceutical Market. To capture world pharmaceutical market, we need world class competent pharmacist in the area of Manufacturing, Research and Development, Quality Assurance and Marketing. We are very happy that Shri Mahesh Mohini College of Pharmacy is committed to provide efficient infrastructure, laboratories, equipment and to develop the students as competent pharmacists and highly qualified dedicated professionals in the future. We will continuously work to improvise the concept of Pharma education in today's global era with an aim to serve humanity and education better.

Our Vision

"Our commitment of ethical teaching practices, stress upon imparting relevant, superior practical skills and knowledge, makes us a premier institute in the Puranpur, Pilibhit, and adjacent region. Rigorous academic schedule, supplemented by exposure to latest developments via guest lectures and industry visits, ensure the making of a perfect pharmacist. Thus, generated workforce would be illuminating the lives of the sufferings by their deeds, world over"

Our Mission

With the moto

तन्मे मनः शिवसंकल्पमास्तु

We are focused" to deliver ethical minded, adaptable pharmacists aiming to improve the quality of life of the human beings" by dedicating ourselves to achieve, sustain and foster unmatched excellence in Technical Education. To this end, we will pursue continuous development of infrastructure and enhance state-of-the art equipments to provide our students a technologically up-to-date and intellectually inspiring environment of learning, research, creativity, innovation and professional activity and inculcate in them ethical and moral values.

Facilities Provided

Class Room

Neat, clean, highly ventilated class room equipped with both traditional and modern techniques of teaching for ease learning

Library

Situated at peaceful, highly ventilated location at the college premises with well-furnished systemically organized.Our library has more than 1500 text book

Laboratories

According to guidelines of PCI, the college premises has hygienic, well ventilated spacious comfortable laboratories as per specialization of pharmacy curriculum with all modern equipment

Computer Lab

SMMMCP's Computer Lab is packed with individual 30 computers all with latest configurations along with English language learning resources. Computer facility is made available for all students and staff. All the staff has been provided dedicated PC's with common printer facility and connected with free internet facility.

Auditorium

SMMMCP Campus has a unique facility of centrally air-conditioned Auditorium equipped with all modern requirements such as high-class audio system and stage. It has seating capacity of 250 persons. The auditorium is used for seminars, guest lectures and cultural activities

Medical Facility

In the College premises, we provide FREE medical First-aid services to all. In case of emergency, we are providing free transportation facility and medical help through Smt Bharti Singh Memorial Hospital, Puranpur located 5 km far from the SMMMCP Campus.

Cafeteria

SMMCP Campus has hygienic, high ventilated and well-furnished common cafeteria to serve hygienic healthy food and soft beverages for all at economic rate during routine college working time.

Sports Complex

To make and maintain a healthy physique, SMMMCP Campus provides a neat, clean, green and peaceful ground to play various outdoor games in all seasons. Common Sports Complex provides all types of tools of indoor games to all under the observation of Sports In-charge Officer.

Transport Facility

The College is situated on NH-730 is approx. 5 km far from Puranpur Bus Station, 5 km Purnapur Railway Station and 35 km from District Headquarter Pilibhit Railway /Bus Station. The College Campus is well connected by Road transport.
