Goal of The Program

D.Pharm -

The diploma in Pharmacy is a two year program which is based on Annual examination system affiliated to Board of Technical education Lucknow. It has been designed to prepare candidate to work as Medical Practitioner by providing a Pharmacist License issued by PCI to run a medical store

Eligibility -

Pass in final examination of 10+2 or equilvalent (with 50% marks in PCM/Bio/PCBio-tech) from a recognized board
