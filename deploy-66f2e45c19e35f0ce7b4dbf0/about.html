About

Built With Greatness Inside

In a peaceful, green, hygienic environment, Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial College of Pharmacy is situated on NH-730, 5 Kilometre stone to Puranpur and is well connected to all the means of transport. The campus offers an excellent infrastructure and highly experienced teaching staff. In the environment of rapidly changing technology, polarized with global competitive intelligence, Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial College of Pharmacy is established in 2020 under Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial Educational Foundation Trust, Puranpur through the painstaking efforts of Hon'ble Chairman Dr. Tej Bahadur Singh. The institute is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) and affiliated Board of Technical Education (BTE) Lucknow. Institute offer Two year Full time Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) The SMMM College of Pharmacy firmly believes in its strength and tries to remain at the forefront of Pharmaceutical education and research by manifesting excellence and meeting the challenges of global competitive environment.

Building a Bright Future Through Education

Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial College is an innovative and dynamic institution committed to providing a transformative educational experience for students. As a newly established college, Horizon College is driven by a vision to create a learning environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and professional development. This article will outline the key features and advantages that set Horizon College apart as an exceptional choice for students seeking a new college to pursue their higher education.

Modern Infrastructure:

Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial College prides itself on offering a contemporary and state-of-the-art campus that facilitates effective learning. The college features modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, cutting-edge technology, and a resourceful library. The infrastructure is designed to promote collaborative learning, creativity, and critical thinking, ensuring students have access to the best resources to enhance their educational journey.

Experienced and Supportive Faculty:

Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial College is proud to have a dedicated faculty team comprising experienced educators, industry professionals, and subject matter experts. The faculty members are passionate about teaching and are committed to providing a holistic learning experience. They go beyond traditional classroom teaching, engaging students in interactive discussions, practical exercises, and research projects. The faculty also serves as mentors, offering guidance and support to students throughout their academic journey.

Focus on Skill Development:

Recognizing the importance of developing a diverse skill set, Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial College emphasizes the cultivation of practical skills alongside theoretical knowledge. The college integrates skill development programs into the curriculum, offering workshops, seminars, and industry interactions to enhance students' soft skills, communication abilities, critical thinking, and problem-solving capabilities. This comprehensive approach equips students with the tools necessary to succeed in their professional careers.

Student Support Services:

Shri Mahesh Mohini Memorial College recognizes the importance of holistic student development and offers comprehensive support services. These include academic counseling, career guidance, mentoring programs, and personal counseling to ensure students' overall well-being. The college also encourages student involvement in extracurricular activities, clubs, and student organizations, fostering a vibrant and inclusive campus community.
